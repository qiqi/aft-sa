## New minor comments

1. Abstract, l. 48–51: the anchor list lost its conjunction in the Mack
   rewording — "the free-shear growth eigenvalue, the linear-stability onset
   of the Falkner–Skan family, the Blasius $e^N$ envelope; its freestream
   seed…" needs "and" before "the Blasius". Also "its freestream seed" —
   the grammatical antecedent is "The amplification rate", but the seed
   belongs to the model; "the model's freestream seed is set by…" removes the
   ambiguity.
2. l. 637–642 (new well-posedness sentence): the substitution
   $v\to u^{-1/2}v$ is stated *before* the reversed-flow floor, but on
   reversed-flow profiles $u<0$ and $u^{-1/2}$ is undefined unless the floored
   advection is what enters the substitution (as the code does —
   `tab_frozen_slope.py` floors $u$ before the sqrt). One clause fixes it:
   "with the floored advection $u$ throughout" or state the floor first.
3. l. 2047 "All eight solutions are steady and fully converged" — flagged
   under the queued refresh (pass-6 minor 11), listed here only so the number
   ("eight" vs the 24-case suite) is not forgotten when the sweep text is
   rewritten.
